Prem mandir;Vrindavan

Prem mandir (the temple of divine love) is a hindu temple in Vrindavan,Mathura district,Western Uttar pradesh,India.The temple was established by Jagadguru Shri Kripalu Ji Maharaj.It is maintained by Jagadguru Kripalu Parishant,an international non -profit,educational,spiritual,charitable trust.It is dedicated to Radha Krishna and sita ram.Radha Krishnaare on thr first level and sita ram are on the seond level.Different leelas of Shri Krishna and Rasik saints are depicted all over the wall of the temple.
